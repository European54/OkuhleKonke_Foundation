Empowering Youth Through Education & Sport

OkuhleKonke Foundation is a community-based charity supporting children and young people who lack access to resources, opportunities, and safe spaces to grow.

What We Stand For

Education Access

We provide school supplies, academic support, and mentorship to learners from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Sports Development

Through organised sports programmes, we promote discipline, teamwork, and healthy lifestyles.

Community Impact

We work hand-in-hand with communities to build long-term, sustainable opportunities for youth.
